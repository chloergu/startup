TripSplit

About TripSplit

TripSplit is a group budgeting app designed to make paying your friends easier when needing to split a multitude of costs. All you need to do is create an account, then join or create a group with a unique name.

I decided to make this web application when I ran into quite a few disputes about money when going on a trip with a group of friends. If we had used TripSplit, it would have cleared up a lot more about money and enabled clearer communication.
