# startup
My startup project for CS260 at BYU

As a kid, one of my favorite websites to play around with was Elf Yourself. This is a website where you could upload a picture of your face, and then the website would insert it into a prerecorded, holiday-themed video. You could even share these videos and send them as animated Christmas cards. However, Elf Yourself is only available during a few weeks over the Christmas season. I want to create a website where you can also upload pictures of your face, and then create my own pictures and possibly videos that would be entertaining to put you and your friend’s faces on. You would also be able to share these creations with others as well with a download button, and even added links for email. This would be fun for everyone, but I’d keep it nice and PG-rated so it’d be a hit for kids especially, like Elf Yourself was for me.
